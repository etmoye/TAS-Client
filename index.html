Purpose of the Review

A 360-Feedback Assessment is a multi-source assessmement in which feedback from an employee's subordinates, colleagues, and supervisor(s), as well as a self-evaluation by the employee themselves is gathered. This tool provides and individual a full understanding of his/her strengths and points of improvement. By assessing our employees on a semi-annual basis, we strive to ensure our team has current feedback to form both short and long term goals. ...
